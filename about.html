HOME AWAY FROM HOME

Born from a Relentless Pursuit of Perfection

Mya Homes & Suites was founded on a singular, unwavering belief: that every traveler deserves an experience that transcends the ordinary. In a city bustling with diplomatic missions, corporate headquarters, and cultural vibrancy, we saw a gap — not just for a place to sleep, but for a sanctuary that feels like home, elevated by the standards of a world-class boutique hotel.

Our growth has been intentional, never compromising on our core values of privacy, security, and impeccable design.

Today, Mya stands as the definitive choice for diplomats, executives, and discerning global citizens who demand more than accommodation — they demand an experience curated to their exacting standards.
